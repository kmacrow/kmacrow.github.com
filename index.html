Welcome

Among other things, I've built Yulan, Asper and Vespen. I'm a trained computer scientist, with a very strong design streak. My background is in systems; operating systems, distributed and parallel computing, networking, virtualization, storage, etc. I have the privilege of working for, and with, some of the best minds in computing in both academic and industrial settings. I have a BSc (and I'm expecting an MSc) in Computer Science from UBC, Vancouver. I was born in 1989, and I've been writing code for the web (re: doing weird things with JavaScript) since 2003.

Prototypes

This page is will a showcase of the UI/UX/design concepts I'm currently working on—some for specific projects, others purely academic, and many just creative brain dumps. The source code for each will be on GitHub. I hope you'll find something interesting, or at least useful, to fork and or use in your own projects.

Philosophy

I like minimalism, and I would describe myself as a minimalist, but I don't advocate applying minimalism to the point of asceticism: shiny, stylish, polished things are fun, and building them is fun. That said, I try and practice a Jobsian attention to detail, and iteration in the pursuit of perfection. I like a complex implementation over a modular one if it yields more performance and or a better experience.
