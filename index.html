Welcome

Among other things, I've built Yulan, Asper and Vespen. I'm a computer scientist by trade, with a very prominent design streak. My background is in systems research; operating systems, distributed and parallel computing, networking, virtualization, storage, etc. I've worked for, and with, some of the best minds in computing in both academic and industrial settings. I have a couple* of degrees in Computer Science from UBC, Vancouver. I was born in 1989, and I've been writing code for the web (re: doing weird things with JavaScript) since 2003.

Prototypes

This page is (will be) a showcase of the UI/UX concepts I'm currently developing—some for specific projects, others purely academic, and many exploratory, experimental or just creative brain dumps. The source code for each unit will be on GitHub, and I hope you'll find something interesting, or at least useful, to fork and or roll into your own projects.

Philosophy

I think minimalism is very powerful, and I would describe myself as a minimalist, but I don't advocate applying minimalism to the point of asceticism: shiny, stylish, polished things are fun and building them is fun. That being said, I do advocate, and try to practice, a Jobsian attention to detail, and iteration in the pursuit of perfection. I favour a complex implementation over a modular one iff achieving performance or the perfect experience demands it.
